Modern Day

A brief overview of the Samsung Galaxy S8 and the Tesla Model S.

Smartphones

The Samsung Galaxy S8 is Samsung's latest flagship smartphone. The Samsung Galaxy S8 possesses an edge to edge SuperAMOLED display, a Dual Pixel 12 megapixel rear facing camera, and an octa core processor.

Transportation

The Tesla Model S is an all electric vehicle that can travel over 300 miles on a single charge. The Tesla Model S can also accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in a mere 2.5 seconds.
